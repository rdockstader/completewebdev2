Welcome to Jon's Vintage Sleds!

Why Choose Jon's Vintage Sleds?

Jon, the owner of Jon's Vintage Sleds, has a long history (see the about section) in the snowmobiling industry. He started young, and has watched and stayed current with the growing snowmobile hobby. Jon hand picks each of the snowmobiles he sales, and the quality for price of his sleds can not be matched. He spends most of his nights looking for the best deals around, and he knows his prices very well. When you shop with Jon, you don't need to worry about anything. You can know with a surety that you're getting the best quality for price of anywhere around.

What does Jon's Vintage Sleds sell?

At Jon's Vintage Sleds, you can find anything from an actual vintage snowmobile to vintage snowmobiling gear. This includes vintage helmets, vintage snowsuits, and even vintage snowmobiling memoriabilia.

How does Jon come by all this classic stuff?

Jon checks all of the major online classifieds, both global and local to find the best prices and the best quality. He checks each item personally and assesses it's quality, and value and prices them accordingly to the rest of the world. He takes the work out of finding good quality items for a good price.
